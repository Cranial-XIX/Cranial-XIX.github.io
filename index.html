Bo Liu

I am a PhD student of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I am very honored to be advised by Prof. Peter Stone and Prof. Qiang Liu. I have a broad interest in reinforcement learning, continual learning, imitation learning and their applications in robotics. Before coming to UT, I was a master student of Computer Science at Stanford University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Fei-Fei Li and Prof. Yuke Zhu on planning under uncertainty, and Prof. Jure Leskovec on massive data mining. I finished my undergraduate study at the Johns Hopkins University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Jason Eisner on generative syntactic parsing.

Email: bliu@cs.utexas.edu
