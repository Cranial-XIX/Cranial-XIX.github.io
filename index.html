I am a Master student of Computer Science at Stanford University. I have a strong interest in neuroscience and machine learning. My current research interest is to develop system and theory that integrate perception, learning and control for general-purpose robots. I am very honored to work with Prof. Fei-Fei Li and Yuke Zhu at Stanford People, AI & Robots Group on robot planning problems. I have also worked with Prof. Jure Leskovec on massive data mining for women's conception prediction.

Prior to Stanford, I received a BSc. degree in Computer Engineering from Johns Hopkins University, where I worked with Prof. Jason Eisner on generative syntactic parsing.
